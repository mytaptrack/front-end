# Adding Team Members

This guide explains how to add new members to a student's support team in mytaptrackÂ®.

## Overview

Team members can include:
- Teachers
- Support staff
- Administrators
- Service providers
- Family members

## Adding a New Team Member

1. Navigate to Team tab
2. Click "Add Team Member"
3. Enter member information:
   - Email address
   - Role assignment
   - Access permissions
4. Send invitation

### Role Assignment

Available roles may include:
- Primary Teacher
- Support Staff
- Administrator
- Service Provider
- Observer

### Access Permissions

Configure access to:
- Behavior tracking
- Report viewing
- Data entry
- Support changes

## Invitation Process

1. System sends email invitation
2. Member creates account/signs in
3. Accepts team invitation
4. Gains specified access

## Best Practices

1. **Team Setup**
   - Clear role definitions
   - Appropriate permissions
   - Documented responsibilities
   - Communication protocol

2. **Communication**
   - Inform existing team
   - Set expectations
   - Share guidelines
   - Provide training

3. **Management**
   - Regular access review
   - Update permissions
   - Monitor activity
   - Document changes

## Security Considerations

### Data Privacy
